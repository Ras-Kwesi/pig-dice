Let's Play Pig Dice

Welcome to pig dice, a 2-player highly interactive game. It takes two to tango, also does it on pig-dice. But first before we dance, this are the rules of engagement :
  • Each player has a turn to role their dice,one turn has three plays, after which it's the other players turn
  • You can role from the numbers 1 to 6.
  • Every play score between 2 to 6 will be added to your turn score which is a total of three plays
  • A play score of 1 ends your turn and results to a Nil
